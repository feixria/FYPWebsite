About

WHEN INFORMATION IS INCONSISTENT AND SCARCE

We at ILL ensures that data conform to up-to-date standards with information that are readily available to the public. Simplifies data collection and processing. Making chest-worn sensor data freely accessible. Breeding data in a variety of format (Accelerometer, Illumination and Postural) to aid in current and future research.

How it works

Become a participant in 4 easy steps!

Sign up for free

Sign up by filling up a Demographic form and taking a Chronotype questionnaire.

Delivery of sensor

After an inspection on the user, the respective sensors will be delivered to your registered address.

Collection of data

Procedures will be provided to guide the participant through the entire data collection process.

Upload data

After 7 days, an ILL operator will personally contact the participant to retrieve the sensors.

Services

WHAT WE OFFER

Simple and Centralized

We provide an easy to use interface to facilitate communication between participants and ILL operators. Information regarding methodology practices is publicly available. Ensuring upload and download processes are fast and efficient.

Consistency and Reproducibility

We only collect data that is most sought after in this field of research. Our Data Processing pipeline has been formulated through intensive review of past research efforts. Rectifying inconsistent methodology and eliminating non-proprietary practices.

Innovation

We at ILL strive to improve on current Actigraphy practices. Proposing a solution to potentially replace commerically available wrist-worn sensor with a more stylish alternative that provides Accelerometer, Illumination and Postural data all in one neat package.
